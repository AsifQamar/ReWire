Every gram counts. Your reward is calculated based on the type, weight, and condition of your e-waste — transparently, on the spot.

Instant credit, no waiting

It's not just the right thing to do — it's the law.

Under India's E-Waste Management Rules 2022, disposing of electronics in regular trash is illegal. These rules mandate strict Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) quotas, creating a massive, government-backed market for organised e-waste collection. ReWire is your easiest path to compliance — and to earning from it.
